So, Why Doing This Check-In Right Now?

In a world where we spend more and more time behind our screens, it’s easy to fall into the trap of endless scrolling—also known as doomscrolling. This constant flood of information can drain us and negatively affect our mental well-being. But instead of getting lost in the digital void, we can use that screen time to check in with ourselves.

A daily check-in is a small yet powerful way to pause, reflect on how we’re feeling, and move forward with intention. It helps us not only focus on what’s happening on the screen but also on what’s happening within us. This way, we can avoid getting caught up in the never-ending stream of content and give ourselves the care and attention we deserve.
